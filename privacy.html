Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Sandpiper ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Sandpiper mobile application and website (the "Service"). We take your privacy seriously and collect only what we need to provide the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Email address. Required to create an account and send your one-time sign-in code. We do not collect passwords.
  • Flight information. Flight numbers and departure dates you enter to generate your jet lag schedule. This is associated with your account to store your trip history.

Information collected automatically

  • Session token. A randomly generated token issued when you sign in, stored securely on your device and transmitted with each authenticated request. Tokens expire after 30 days of inactivity.
  • Usage logs. Basic server logs including request timestamps, API endpoints accessed, and error events. These logs do not include the content of your schedule or personal health decisions. Logs are retained for a limited period for debugging and security purposes.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your precise or approximate location.
  • We do not collect health records, biometrics, or any medical data.
  • We do not track your behavior across third-party apps or websites.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers or serve targeted ads.
  • We do not sell your data to any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session.
  • Generate and store your jet lag schedules and trip history.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the Service.

We do not use your information for marketing communications unless you have explicitly opted in. We do not build profiles for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers hosted by Amazon Web Services (AWS) in a region with appropriate data protection practices. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure token handling, and access controls limiting who can access production data.

A portion of your data — your trips, flight details, and schedule events — is also cached locally on your device using the device's secure storage. This cache exists to allow offline access and is cleared when you sign out.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. While we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Services

The Service uses the following third-party infrastructure:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS). Cloud computing and database infrastructure. AWS processes data on our behalf under their data processing agreements.
  • Flight data provider. We query a third-party flight data API using the flight number and date you provide. Only these two values are sent to the provider; your email address and account identity are never shared.
  • Google Fonts. Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts. This involves your browser making a request to Google's servers, which may log your IP address per Google's own privacy policy.

We do not integrate with social networks, advertising networks, or analytics platforms that track you across the web.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account through the app, your email address, session tokens, and all associated trip data are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained indefinitely for analytics and product improvement.

Server logs are retained for a maximum of 90 days.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data including:

  • Access. Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction. Request that inaccurate data be corrected.
  • Deletion. Request deletion of your account and associated data. You can initiate this directly from the app's account settings.
  • Portability. Request your data in a portable format.
  • Objection. Object to certain uses of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@sandpiper-app.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. International Users

The Service is operated from and data is stored in the United States (via AWS). If you access the Service from outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries that may have different data protection laws than your jurisdiction. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

Users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom may have additional rights under the GDPR or UK GDPR. Contact us to exercise those rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at this URL with the updated date shown above. For significant changes, we will notify users via email where feasible. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For questions, concerns, or data requests related to this Privacy Policy, please reach out at hello@sandpiper-app.com.
