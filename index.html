The problem

Jet lag steals your best days abroad.

Crossing time zones throws your circadian rhythm out of sync. Your body still thinks it's 3am when the sun is bright. Melatonin fires at the wrong time. You can't sleep when you should, can't stay awake when you need to. Most travelers just wait it out — Sandpiper gives you a plan to correct it fast.

1–1.5 days to adjust per time zone crossed without intervention
Up to 9× faster recovery with timed light & melatonin
48 h the critical window to reprogram your sleep cycle

The schedule

Every action, perfectly timed.

Your schedule spans three phases — pre-flight preparation, in-flight guidance, and post-arrival recovery. Each card is specific, actionable, and grounded in circadian science.

Pre-flight
SEEK LIGHT

Get bright morning light

Go outside or sit near a window. Morning light is your most powerful tool to anchor your circadian rhythm to the destination time zone.
